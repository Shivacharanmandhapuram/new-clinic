# InnovateHealth Design System & Color Guide

This document establishes the visual language and color strategy for InnovateHealth. The aesthetic is designed to immediately communicate **trust, clinical credibility, and operational sophistication**. It distances itself from aggressive, cold "tech-startup" blues in favor of warm, grounded, premium healthcare tones.

---

## 1. Core Brand Ethos
InnovateHealth is "built inside a real clinic, by doctors, for doctors." The interface must reflect this origin. It should feel like a premium, well-managed medical office: clean, calm, deeply trustworthy, with precise moments of high-efficiency energy. 

Our dual-tone approach balances **Clinical Authority** (deep greens, elegant serif text) with **Modern Automation** (vibrant coral accents, clinical whites).

---

## 2. Color Palette Breakdown

### 🟢 Primary Brand Tone: "Clinical Forest"
**Hex:** `#1A332C`
- **Role:** Primary buttons, footer background, key structural elements.
- **Meaning:** Grounded, stable, authoritative. This dark green immediately evokes holistic health, precision, and the calm environment of a premium clinic. It acts as the confident anchor for the entire page.

### 🟠 Action & Automation Accent: "Vitality Coral"
**Hex:** `#E8734A`
- **Role:** Interactive hover states, rotating sub-headlines, key feature highlights, and animated elements.
- **Meaning:** Speed, life, and flow. Coral is the energetic counterbalance to the dark green. We use it sparingly to highlight *where the AI takes action* (e.g., "For faster patient flow", "every time"). It draws the eye without causing the panic often associated with bright red.

---

## 3. Typography & Text Colors

We avoid standard tech-gray (`#333333`) in favor of richer, more sophisticated text tones that pair beautifully with our editorial fonts (`Playfair Display` and `Inter`).

### 🖋️ Primary Text: "Mahogany Black"
**Hex:** `#28030F` *(Inspired by Heidi Health's exact premium styling)*
- **Role:** All main headlines (`h1`, `h2`, `h3`) and prominent UI text.
- **Meaning:** A deep, rich, almost-espresso black. It provides incredibly sharp contrast against light backgrounds while possessing a subtle warmth that pure black lacks. It gives the typography an expensive, "published-journal" feel.

### 📝 Muted Text: "Sage Slate"
**Hex:** `#4A635B`
- **Role:** Paragraphs, sub-notes, secondary descriptions.
- **Meaning:** A muted, desaturated forest green-gray. It lowers reading fatigue for long paragraphs while maintaining color harmony with the primary green brand tone.

---

## 4. Backgrounds & Surfaces

The background system is designed to simulate a clean, sterile, yet welcoming medical environment. We use very subtle, mint-tinted off-whites instead of stark hospital-blinding white.

### ☁️ Base Background: "Clinic Cool"
**Hex:** `#E9F0EC`
- **Role:** The main wrapper for large sections (like the Hero and CTA sections).
- **Meaning:** A very soft, cool sage-gray. It acts as a calming foundation that makes white UI elements (like cards and device mockups) visually "pop" off the screen.

### 🧊 Surface / Card Background: "Sterile Mint"
**Hex:** `#F5FAF7`
- **Role:** The background for floating Bento grid cards, testimonial blocks, and interactive UI panels.
- **Meaning:** Represents the pristine surfaces inside a high-end clinic. It is nearly white, but the 2% mint tint keeps it integrated with the overarching brand palette. 

---

## 5. UI Accents & Dividers

### 🌫️ Boundaries: "Sage Line"
**Hex Variables:** `#C5D5CE` / `rgba(26, 51, 44, 0.1)`
- **Role:** Borders between sections, faint outlines on cards, subtle dividing lines.
- **Meaning:** Provides structure without visual clutter. The slight green tint keeps the dividing lines from looking like harsh, basic gray borders.

### 🌟 Glows & Micro-interactions
- **Coral Glow:** `rgba(232, 115, 74, 0.2)` — Used behind energetic elements or to highlight the "active" state of the AI.
- **Soft Shadow (Depth):** `0 16px 40px -8px rgba(26, 51, 44, 0.10)` — All shadows are tinted with the `Clinical Forest` green rather than pure black, ensuring shadows look like natural ambient light within the brand environment.
